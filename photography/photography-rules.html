timelapse & hyperlapse

ONLINE

Details

Video can be taken in mobile phones or camera. The video limit is around 30secs. Send the drive link of the video with public accessible link.

Rules

* Participants must make sure they have done common registration and add those details in the email, failing to do so will result in disqualification.
* Everyone has to submit their photos and college identification.
* Only one Photo/Video will be accepted per event.
* Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline exceeds.
* Photos submitted must be at least 640 pixels on the shorter side and Photos must be in JPEG format.
* All submitted photos must contain the original EXIF meta data information. However there must be no border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, identifying marks, or any other visible references and/or marks on the image.
* Basic editing, including color enhancement and cropping of the Photo(s) is acceptable,
provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the Photo(s).
* Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions and/or manipulations,
and the adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is prohibited.
* Photos that portray or otherwise include inappropriate and/or offensive content,
including provocative nudity, violence, human rights and/or environmental violation,
and/or any other contents deemed to be contrary to the law, religious, cultural & moral traditions and practices of India,are strictly prohibited and will be immediately discarded.
* The decision of judging panel will be final and participants are to bind by it in respect to the contest.

glimpse of milan street

Offline

Details

You can use only mobile phones. Cameras are not allowed inside the campus. You are allowed to shoot everything that is happening on the streets of Milan except the Pro-shows, Inauguration and Valedictory. Edit the videos with any background songs to make it creative.
Don’t use Apps like Tik Tok, Musically etc, use Professional editing App to get better results. The time limit for the video is 30 secs-1 min.You need to submit this video directly in the room alloted.

Rules

* Participants must make sure the Common registration is done, otherwise photos will be disqualified.
*Everyone has to write their Name, Registration No. Department, College, Place, Contact No. on the backside of your respective photocopies while submitting their photocopies otherwise the photographs will not be considered for judging.
*Submissions will not be accepted once the deadline is exceeded.
*Only one Photo/Video will be accepted per event.
*Photos submitted must be in the Glossy Sheet or Photographic Sheet and come along with the original soft copy of the printed photos for verification in mobile or Pen drive (will be disqualified failing to do so).
*All submitted photos must contain the original EXIF metadata information in your softcopy.
*Make sure your printed photocopy is of good quality.
*Basic editing, including color enhancement and cropping of the Photo(s) is acceptable, provided any such editing does not affect the authenticity and/or genuineness of the Photo(s).
* Advanced editing used to create illusions, deceptions and/or manipulations, adding and removing of significant elements within the frame is prohibited.
*Photos that portray or otherwise include inappropriate and/or offensive content including provocative nudity, violence, human rights and/or environmental violation, and/or any other contents deemed to be contrary to the law, religious, cultural & moral traditions and practices of India, are strictly prohibited and will be immediately discarded.
*The decision of judging panel will be final and participants are to bind by it in respect to the contest. 
*The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.
